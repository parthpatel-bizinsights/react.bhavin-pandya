# Spiritual Energy Solutions for Motel Business - Landing Page

## Design Guidelines

### Design References
- **Luxury spiritual wellness sites**: Calm, trustworthy, professional
- **High-converting landing pages**: Clear CTAs, social proof, minimal distractions
- **Style**: Modern Minimal + Luxury Spiritual + Professional Business

### Color Palette
- Primary Deep Blue: #1B2A4A (trust, depth)
- Secondary Gold: #C9A84C (prosperity, warmth)
- Light Gold: #F5E6B8 (subtle accents)
- White: #FFFFFF (purity, clarity)
- Off-White: #F8F6F0 (warm backgrounds)
- Dark Text: #1A1A2E (readability)
- Muted Text: #6B7280 (secondary text)
- Light Blue: #E8EDF5 (section backgrounds)

### Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (elegant serif) - weight 700
- Body: Inter (modern sans-serif) - weight 400/500
- Accent/CTA: Inter - weight 600/700

### Key Component Styles
- **Buttons Primary**: Gold background (#C9A84C), dark text (#1B2A4A), rounded-lg, hover: brighten
- **Buttons Secondary**: Transparent with gold border, gold text, hover: gold bg
- **Cards**: White bg, subtle shadow, rounded-xl, gold accent border on hover
- **Sections**: Alternating white and off-white backgrounds
- **Hero**: Full viewport, dark overlay on image, white text
